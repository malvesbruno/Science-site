  • Not Perfectly Round

    Earth is not a perfect sphere; it's slightly flattened at the poles and bulges at the equator

  • Liquid Water

    Earth is the only known planet in our solar system with liquid water on the surface

  • Unique Name

    Unlike other planets in our solar system, Earth's name does not come from Greek or Roman mythology; it's derived from Old English and Germanic meaning "the ground"

  • Life's Cradle

    Earth's vast oceans and hospitable temperatures made it a cradle for life to begin around 3.8 billion years ago

  • The Moon's Influence

    The Moon is slowly drifting away from Earth, affecting our planet's tides, rotation, and even the length of our days
